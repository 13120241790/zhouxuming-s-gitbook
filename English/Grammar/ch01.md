句子的构成

五大基本句型，简单句：

- 主谓 I cry
- 主谓宾 I love you 
- 主谓双宾 I gave an apple 
- 主谓宾补 I made you happy 
- 主系表 I am a teacher

其他都是修饰成分

The boy gave the girl a box (主谓双宾)


冠词 The  a

形容词  tall
The tall boy gave the girl a box 


副词 very
The very tall boy gave the girl a box 

介词 with a hat （介词短语）

The very tall boy with a hat gave the girl a box 

非谓语动词

The very tall boy with a hat made of grass gave the girl a box
